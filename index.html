Game Rules (Rules 1.0–11.0)
1.0All games governed by SPN rules except where HCCSL rules specifically override.
2.0Teams must have a minimum of 8 players (including at least 3 women) to start a game. Fewer than 8 = forfeit.
2.1Maximum of 10 players on the field. Batting order may include up to 16 players.
3.0Gender ratio: At least 3 women in the field at all times. If a team cannot field 3 women, an out is recorded each time the missing spot comes to bat.
4.0All players must be registered with SPN and listed on the team's official roster before playing.
5.0Pitching arc: Minimum 6 feet, maximum 12 feet from the ground.
6.0Home run limit: 3 over-the-fence HR per team per game. Additional over-fence hits count as a single.
7.0Mercy rule: 15 runs after 3 innings, 12 runs after 5 innings.
8.0Weather cancellation: Game suspended due to weather before becoming official = rescheduled. If official = result stands. Suspended official game = 7–7 tie.
9.0A regulation game is 7 innings. Tied games after 7 innings in the regular season end as a tie.
10.0Courtesy runner: Each team may use one courtesy runner per inning. Runner must be the last recorded out.
11.0No new inning shall start after 1 hour 45 minutes from the official start time (curfew). Existing inning completes.
Standings (Rule 10.0)
10.0Points: Win = 2 pts, Tie = 1 pt, Loss = 0 pts.
10.1Tiebreaker order: (a) head-to-head points, (b) winner of most recent matchup, (c) coin toss.
10.2Run differential capped at +7 per game for standings purposes.
10.3CrossOver is a guest team — excluded from standings. Results vs CrossOver count toward league teams' records.
Curfew & Official Games (Rules 11.0–12.1)
11.0Curfew: no new inning after 1h 45m. 15-min grace period if both teams and umpire agree (counts toward 1h 45m total). Game reverts to last complete inning if time expires.
12.1Official game = 3½ innings (home team leading) or 4 complete innings.
Playoffs (Rules 13.0–13.4)
13.0Two PODs — POD A (top 5) and POD B (bottom 4).
13.2All playoff games: single game, 7 innings, 12-run mercy after 5 innings.
13.3Playoff tie after 7 innings → international tiebreaker extra innings.
13.4Playoff eligibility: 40% attendance (8 of 20 games). Roster submitted by Aug 27.
Batting Order & Substitutions (Rules 15–16)
15.0Provide lineup to opposing team before each game. Attendance sheets to league within 48 hours.
16.0Late-arriving players must be added to the bottom of the batting order or sit out.
16.2Substitute players declared at game start. Late arrivals only added if order has not been completed once.
16.4Mid-game substitutions must be gender-for-gender unless batting order allows otherwise.
16.5Pinch runners: max 3 per game.
Conduct & Ejections (Rules 17–19)
17.0Zero tolerance for harassment, discrimination, or abusive language. Immediate ejection. Repeat = suspension.
17.1Ejected player must leave the field and surrounding area. Team plays short.
18.0Alcohol on the diamond or dugout = ejection. Permitted in designated areas only.
18.1Coaches responsible for spectator conduct. Failure to control spectators can result in ejection or forfeit.
19.0Games must be officiated by at least 1 umpire. No umpire = game cancelled.
